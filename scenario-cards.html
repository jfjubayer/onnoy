📋 Scenario Cards

প্রিন্টযোগ্য কার্ড

Print these cards for your classroom session. Cut them out and hand one to each group.

Card 01
🌐 Digital Responsibility

Your friend forwards a WhatsApp message saying "URGENT: A new virus is spreading in Mymensingh — avoid eating street food." It has been shared 500 times. What do you do?

Card 02
🌐 Digital Responsibility

A photo of a classmate taken at a private moment is being shared in your class group chat. People are laughing. The classmate doesn't know yet. What do you do?

Card 03
📱 Screen Time

You have an exam tomorrow. It's 9pm. You've studied for 2 hours. Your phone keeps buzzing with notifications. What is your plan for the next 3 hours?

Card 04
🌐 Digital Responsibility

You've been watching YouTube for 2 hours. You didn't plan to — autoplay kept going. Who is responsible — you or YouTube? What do you do next time?

Card 05
🌐 Digital Responsibility

A classmate is being mocked in a Facebook group by people from your school. You don't participate but you don't say anything either. Are you responsible?

Card 06
✏️ Academic Honesty

You have a Bangladesh Studies assignment due tomorrow. You find a perfect paragraph on Wikipedia. What are your options and what should you do?

Card 07
📱 Screen Time

Your screen time report says you spent 4 hours on social media today. You feel like you wasted the day. What one change could you make tomorrow?

Card 08
✏️ Academic Honesty

Your friend asks to copy your homework — they promise to change the words. They say "everyone does it." How do you respond?
