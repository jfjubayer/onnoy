Misinformation Red Flags

মিথ্যা তথ্য চেনার উপায়

Read a post. Go through 8 questions. Find out if it's likely real or fake.

Read the post or message you received. Then answer these 8 questions honestly.
Does the headline use ALL CAPS or excessive exclamation marks???
Does it say "SHARE IMMEDIATELY" or create panic urgency?
Is there no author name or source mentioned?
Does the website URL look strange or misspelled?
Does it make an extraordinary claim with no evidence?
Does it trigger strong emotion — anger, fear, or disgust?
Can you NOT find this story on any trusted news site?
Does it ask you to share before reading the full content?
