About Onnoy

অন্বয় — Connecting necessary dots

Who we are

Onnoy is a youth-led initiative based in Mymensingh, Bangladesh. We visit colleges and take 90-minute sessions with Grade 11–12 students on the ethical use of technology. Our sessions are free. Our goal is awareness, not lectures.

What we do

🏫

We Go to Schools

We travel to colleges across Mymensingh and beyond — bringing the session to students where they already are.

🎤

We Run Sessions

Our 90-minute sessions are interactive, story-driven, and led by young facilitators who speak the same language as students.

📈

We Measure Impact

After every session we collect feedback and track how student awareness changes over time.

Our framework

Our curriculum is aligned with the UNESCO Digital Competency Framework and ISTE Digital Citizenship Standards.
